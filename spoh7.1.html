Superior Potion of Healing

Like I said, we’ve been in some disgusting places, and the lodgings we find ourselves in afterwards don’t exactly have hot showers. Learning to adapt and make ourselves feel refreshed is the key, and I’d bet that those strategies will help you as well.

If you haven’t already done so today, take a shower and change your clothes. Some other things you can do are put on lotion, wash your face, change into clean clothes, or any other self-care routine you enjoy.
