Superior Potion of Healing

I’m sorry to hear that you’re in pain. Unfortunately, Healing Word only has a range of 60 feet, so I can’t heal you directly. However, you might have the tools to deal with the pain yourself, so just hang in there until we can get it sorted out.

If you have a medication that your doctor has prescribed or recommended for your pain, you should take that. For aches and pains, try an aspirin or a heating or ice pack. For a stomach ache, try medication or hot tea. You can also try to distract yourself from the pain with a pleasant alternative sensation, like good smells and pleasurable textures. Overall, listen to your body and be patient with it.
