Superior Potion of Healing

You know, sometimes, I don’t realize how much I need to go into stasis mode until I actually do it. That may not be the case for you, but I want you to consider it, okay? I don’t want you to gain a level of exhaustion.

Even if you’re not tired, it’s important to get enough sleep for your mind and body to repair itself. Try turning off all lights and electronics and lay in bed or another comfortable spot. If, after fifteen or twenty minutes, you haven’t fallen asleep, you can get up again; no pressure. Over the next few days, keep an eye on your habits so you can get some sleep: try to exercise, reduce caffeine, and work on reducing stress or adrenaline that may be preventing you from sleeping.
