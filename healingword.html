Healing Word

A lot of these may seem obvious, but they can be difficult for plenty of soul-touched people. So, use this list to get your gears turning.

  • drink water
  • eat some food
  • take a walk
  • dance to upbeat music
  • get a small task done
  • give someone a genuine compliment
  • give yourself a genuine compliment
  • take a shower and get dressed
  • take a nap
  • be patient with yourself

Don't forget to love each other; don't forget to love yourself.
