Superior Potion of Healing

I’ve noticed that Imogen likes apples, while Fearne likes berries or… steak with gravy, for some reason? She was raving all about it one night. Also, she was telling us about these scones her grandmother made and they sounded really good. What do you like to eat?

You have a couple of options when it comes to food. If you just need a snack, you could have

  • chips,
  • pretzels,
  • slices of apples,
  • popcorn,
  • pieces of chocolate - whatever you're feeling like.

For a full meal, if you don’t feel like cooking, you can get take-out or eat out. However, if you’d rather cook, some low-effort foods include

  • eggs,
  • salad,
  • ramen,
  • pasta,
  • mac and cheese,
  • and sandwiches.

Whether you’re snacking or having a full meal, don’t worry about whether or not it’s perfectly healthy; the important thing at the moment is giving yourself some fuel, so trust your body. Cooking can be an enjoyable activity to some people, so if that includes you, go all out and make your favorite dish. Or, if you’re looking to try your hand at something new, check out Crit Snacks.
