Superior Potion of Healing

I’ve noticed that Imogen likes apples, while Fearne likes berries or… steak with gravy, for some reason? She was raving all about it one night. Also, she was telling us about these scones her grandmother made and they sounded really good. What do you like to eat?

Even though you’re not hungry, you might want to consider eating at some point, either now or in the next couple of hours. Food is essential to keeping up your energy and helping you build a routine. Consider snacking on a food you enjoy:

  • chips,
  • pretzels,
  • slices of apples,
  • popcorn,
  • pieces of chocolate - whatever you're feeling like.

Don’t worry about whether or not it’s perfectly healthy; the important thing at the moment is giving yourself some fuel.
