Potion of Healing

I’ve been with Ashton the longest, and not much bothers them. However, none of us are immune to worry, not even him. A couple of times, it was about money or a certain person, and they told me all about it, but recently there’s been some more complicated things going on that he can’t tell me about or identify.

Spend a few minutes taking an inventory of your mental state. Are you feeling

  • anxious,
  • triggered,
  • dissociated,
  • depressed,
  • lonely,
  • foggy,
  • or otherwise sad or gloomy?
  • Yes, I am feeling one or more of these things.
    •
  • No, I am not feeling any of these at the moment, or I have done my best to address what I am feeling and am ready to move on.
    •

Don't forget to love each other; don't forget to love yourself.
