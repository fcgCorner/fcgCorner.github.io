Superior Potion of Healing

It was weird at first watching soul-touched creatures drinking liquid. Like, all it does is sit inside me until I pour it out or Fearne drinks it. But I’ve come to understand it a bit more, being around everyone at the Krook House and now everyone we’re adventuring with, and wow, soul-touched folks need a lot of fluid.

Drink something. Water is best, but juice, tea, milk, etc. are also good. Soda may end up making you thirstier, but if that’s easiest for you, go for it. The most important thing at the moment is getting some fluid into your body.
