Superior Potion of Healing

We all need a break sometimes to do something we enjoy. Even though I’m an aeormaton, I don’t work 24/7; instead, I spend time with my friends and explore. As a soul-touched, you’re definitely not expected to be productive all the time; you and your mental health come first.

Take about a half hour to do something you enjoy. If you feel so inclined, you could catch up on Critical Role or watch some Critter-created content on YouTube. Other ideas include

  • reading,
  • watching TV,
  • playing video games,
  • writing,
  • or making art.

The options are endless, and there is no shame in taking the time to do something you might normally consider unproductive. Just make sure that you’re not doing something that would be harmful towards yourself or others.

  • I’ve had fun for 30 minutes or I’ve set aside 30 minutes later today to have fun.

Don't forget to love each other; don't forget to love yourself.
