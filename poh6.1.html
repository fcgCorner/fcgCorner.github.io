Potion of Healing

I want you to be comfortable in your environment, so we’re going to work it out together. Hopefully, we can brainstorm and come up with a solution that works for you.

Take a minute to figure out what in your environment is making you uncomfortable. Physical discomforts, like being too hot or cold or being in a messy space, are fairly easy to fix. If you feel overwhelmed or unsafe in your environment, it’s probably best to relocate. If you can’t relocate, be patient with yourself and try distracting yourself by playing music with headphones.
