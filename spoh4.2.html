Superior Potion of Healing

I totally understand; sometimes, I think I need to go into stasis mode, but then after doing so I don’t feel any better. In those cases, there’s usually something else I need to do for my body to operate correctly.

If you’ve gotten enough sleep but you’re still tired, more sleep is not always the solution. Take a minute to sit and listen to your body. If you’re hungry, grab something to eat. If you haven’t exercised recently, getting physical activity may increase your energy. If there isn’t a physical explanation for feeling tired, your mood might be affecting your energy level, and we’ll address that shortly.
