Potion of Healing

The other day, Fearne was telling us about these scones her grandmother made and they sounded really good. What do you like to eat?

If you only have time to have a snack right now, that’s alright! Some snack ideas are

  • chips,
  • pretzels,
  • apple slices,
  • popcorn,
  • or pieces of chocolate.

If you have time for a full meal, that’s even better. Whether you’re snacking or having a full meal, don’t worry about whether or not it’s perfectly healthy; the important thing at the moment is giving yourself some fuel, so trust your body.
