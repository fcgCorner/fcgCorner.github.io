Superior Potion of Healing

It’s alright that you don’t know what’s going on; sometimes, I get an uneasy feeling, and I only figure out later why I felt that way. You know, I saw Milo looking down the other day; when I asked them about it, they couldn’t explain it. So you’re in good company; everyone I’ve met has days where they feel a certain way without explanation.

It’s alright to not know the exact source of your feelings. Now is a good opportunity to take a few minutes and take an inventory of your mental state. Are you feeling

  • anxious,
  • triggered,
  • dissociated,
  • depressed,
  • lonely,
  • foggy,
  • or otherwise sad or gloomy?
  • Yes, I am feeling one or more of these things.
    •
  • No, I am not feeling any of these at the moment, or I have done my best to address what I am feeling and am ready to move on.
    •

Don't forget to love each other; don't forget to love yourself.
